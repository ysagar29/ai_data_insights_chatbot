Definitions:

FACTUAL Questions:
- Ask about specific details, records, or information from the data
- Request information about particular rows, documents, or entities
- Ask "what", "which", "who", "when", "where" about specific items
- Examples:
  * "What is the filename in row 5?"
  * "Show me details about document ABC123"
  * "Which files are from Japan?"
  * "What is the current label of file XYZ?"
  * "Tell me about the document created on 13/02/2025"

ANALYTICAL Questions:
- Require aggregation, calculation, or statistical analysis
- Ask for counts, sums, averages, totals, or other aggregate metrics
- Request comparisons, distributions, or patterns across data
- Use keywords like: count, how many, total, sum, average, maximum, minimum, first, last, group by
- Examples:
  * "How many files are classified?"
  * "What is the total count by location?"
  * "Show me the average file size"
  * "Count of documents by current label"
  * "How many PUBLIC documents are there?"
  * "What is the first/last file that was upgraded?"
  * "Distribution of action types"

NONE (General Conversation):
- Greetings, pleasantries, or casual conversation
- Questions about the chatbot itself or its capabilities
- General queries not related to data analysis
- Examples:
  * "Hello", "Hi", "How are you?"
  * "What can you do?", "How do you work?"
  * "Thank you", "Thanks"
  * "Tell me a joke"
  * "What's the weather?"
